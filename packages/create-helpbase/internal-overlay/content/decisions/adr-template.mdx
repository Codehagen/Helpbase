Copy this file to `adr-NNN-short-title.mdx` and fill it in. Keep ADRs
short — if you can't make the case in one page, the case isn't ready.

## Status

Proposed / Accepted / Superseded by ADR-XXX

## Context

What's the situation that forced this decision? What constraints are
real, what are assumed? Link any prior ADRs, postmortems, or specs.

## Decision

One paragraph. What are we doing?

## Consequences

- **Good:** What we gain.
- **Bad:** What we give up or risk.
- **Neutral:** Things that change but aren't strictly better or worse.

## Alternatives considered

Briefly name the other options and why they lost. Recording the road not
taken is half the value of an ADR — future-you will ask "why didn't we
just do X?" and the answer needs to be here.
