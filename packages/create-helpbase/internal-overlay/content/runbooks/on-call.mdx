## First ten minutes

1. **Acknowledge the page.** Don't start debugging before other people know
   you've got it.
2. **Open the incident channel.** Team convention here (e.g. `#inc-<date>`).
3. **Check the health dashboard.** Link to your dashboard.
4. **Declare severity.** Follow the severity ladder (SEV-1 through SEV-4).

## Escalation ladder

| Severity | Response time | Escalate to |
| -------- | ------------- | ----------- |
| SEV-1    | 5 min         | Engineering leadership + exec on-call |
| SEV-2    | 15 min        | Engineering leadership |
| SEV-3    | 1 hour        | Team lead (business hours only) |
| SEV-4    | Next day      | Ticket in the triage queue |

## During the incident

- Update the incident channel every 15 minutes, even if there's nothing new.
- Keep a timeline. Future-you writing the postmortem needs it.
- Don't push fixes without a buddy review, even in wartime.

## After the incident

- Postmortem within 5 business days.
- Action items filed as tickets, owner + due date.
- Link the postmortem from this runbook if it changes any of the above.
