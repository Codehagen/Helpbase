# Introduction

Welcome to your help center! This is your first article, created automatically when you scaffolded this project.

## What is this?

This is a developer-first help center built with Next.js and MDX. You write articles in markdown, and they render as a polished help center.

<Callout type="tip">
You can use rich MDX components like this callout, step-by-step guides, accordions, tabs, and card grids to make your articles more useful.
</Callout>

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit this file">
    Open `content/getting-started/introduction.mdx` in your editor. This is the article you're reading right now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new articles">
    Add `.mdx` files to the `content/` directory. Organize them into categories using subdirectories.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See your changes live">
    Run `pnpm dev` to start the development server. Changes to your articles will appear instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Writing articles

Every article needs frontmatter at the top:

```yaml
---
schemaVersion: 1
title: "Your Article Title"
description: "A brief description"
tags: ["tag1", "tag2"]
order: 1
featured: false
---
```
